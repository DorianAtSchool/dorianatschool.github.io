About Me

I'm a computer science student at the University of Massachusetts Amherst with a passion for AI safety, alignment, and interpretability. I'm currently advised by Prof. Shlomo Zilberstein @ the Resource-Bounded Reasoning Lab, where I currently work on multi-agent Vision-Language-Action systems.
